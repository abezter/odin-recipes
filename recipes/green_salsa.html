Green Salsa

Salsa verde is a type of spicy, green sauce in Mexican cuisine based on tomatillo and green chili peppers.

The tomatillo-based Mexican salsa verde dates to the Aztec Empire, as documented by the Spanish physician Francisco Hern√°ndez.

Only the spiciest serrano peppers should be used.

Ingredients

  • Green tomatillo
  • Serrano Peppers/Jalapeno Peppers
  • Cilantro
  • Onion
  • salt to taste

Recipe instructions

  1. Bring water to a roaring boil
  2. Add tomatillo's, Serrano Peppers, Jalapeno Peppers, and Onion
  3. Let boil for 10 min
  4. Drain and place all ingredients into a blender
  5. Add cilantro and salt to the blender
  6. Blend
